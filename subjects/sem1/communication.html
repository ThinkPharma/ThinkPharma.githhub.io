📘 PCI Syllabus

Unit 1

Communication - An Introduction

6 Hours
  • Definition and process of communication
  • Types of communication - verbal, non-verbal, written
  • Barriers to communication
  • Overcoming communication barriers
  • Importance of communication in pharmacy
Unit 2

Verbal & Non-Verbal Communication

6 Hours
  • Elements of verbal communication
  • Tone, pitch and clarity
  • Body language and gestures
  • Facial expressions and eye contact
  • Personal space and posture
Unit 3

Listening & Reading Skills

6 Hours
  • Types of listening - active, passive, selective
  • Barriers to effective listening
  • Improving listening skills
  • Reading techniques - skimming, scanning
  • Comprehension and retention
Unit 4

Writing Skills

6 Hours
  • Principles of effective writing
  • Letter writing - formal and informal
  • Report writing and documentation
  • Resume and curriculum vitae
  • Email etiquette
Unit 5

Presentation & Interview Skills

6 Hours
  • Planning and preparing presentations
  • Visual aids and their use
  • Overcoming stage fear
  • Interview preparation and techniques
  • Group discussions and debates
