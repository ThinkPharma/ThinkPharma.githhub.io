5 Units

📘 PCI Syllabus

Unit 1

Impurities in Pharmaceuticals

10 Hours
  • Sources of impurities in pharmaceuticals
  • Types of impurities
  • Limit tests for chlorides, sulphates
  • Limit tests for iron, heavy metals, arsenic
  • Modified limit tests
Unit 2

Acids, Bases & Buffers

8 Hours
  • Definition of acids, bases and salts
  • Buffers and buffer capacity
  • Buffer equation and applications
  • Antacids - systemic and non-systemic
  • Acidifying and alkalizing agents
Unit 3

Major Intra & Extra Cellular Electrolytes

9 Hours
  • Physiological ions and their functions
  • Electrolytes used in therapy
  • Oral rehydration salt (ORS)
  • Physiological acid-base balance
  • Electrolyte replacement therapy
Unit 4

Dental Products

8 Hours
  • Dentifrices - composition and types
  • Role of fluoride in dental health
  • Desensitizing agents
  • Anticaries agents
  • Dental cements
Unit 5

Gastrointestinal Agents

10 Hours
  • Antacids - classification and mechanism
  • Cathartics - types and uses
  • Antimicrobials - GI tract
  • Protectives and adsorbents
  • Antidiarrheal agents
