📘 PCI Syllabus

Unit 1

Nervous System

10 Hours
  • Organization of nervous system
  • Neuron - structure and types
  • Nerve impulse - generation and transmission
  • Central nervous system - brain and spinal cord
  • Peripheral nervous system
Unit 2

Cardiovascular System

10 Hours
  • Heart - anatomy and physiology
  • Cardiac cycle and heart sounds
  • Blood pressure and its regulation
  • Electrocardiogram (ECG)
  • Blood vessels - arteries, veins, capillaries
Unit 3

Respiratory System

8 Hours
  • Respiratory organs and their functions
  • Mechanism of respiration
  • Gas exchange and transport
  • Regulation of respiration
  • Lung volumes and capacities
Unit 4

Urinary System

9 Hours
  • Kidney - structure and functions
  • Nephron - structure and urine formation
  • Regulation of renal function
  • Ureters, urinary bladder, urethra
  • Micturition reflex
Unit 5

Endocrine System & Reproductive System

8 Hours
  • Endocrine glands and their hormones
  • Mechanism of hormone action
  • Male reproductive system
  • Female reproductive system
  • Menstrual cycle and pregnancy
